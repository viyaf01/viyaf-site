Privacy Notes / Datenschutzhinweis (GDPR) / 隐私保护要点

This website is a static information page: it sets no tracking cookies and runs no third-party analytics scripts; page fonts are loaded via Google Fonts, which involves a technical request from your browser to that service. Personal data submitted when you contact us by email (name, email address, organisation, message content) is used solely to handle your request and subsequent communication, following data-minimisation and retention-period principles, and is never sold or used for unrelated purposes. Identity and source-of-funds information relating to donations is processed under statutory KYC/AML requirements. The capture, storage and publication of imagery follows GDPR, child-protection and written-consent procedures, with a withdrawal mechanism. To exercise your rights of access, rectification or erasure, please contact office@viyaf.org.

本网站为静态信息页面，不设置跟踪 Cookie，不运行第三方分析脚本；页面字体经由 Google Fonts 加载，浏览器会向该服务发起技术性请求。您通过邮件与我们联系时提交的个人数据（姓名、邮箱、机构、来函内容），仅用于处理您的请求与后续沟通，遵循数据最小化与留存周期原则，不会出售或用于无关用途。涉及捐赠的身份与资金来源信息按 KYC/AML 法定要求处理。影像的采集、存储与公开遵循 GDPR、未成年人保护与书面授权程序，并设有撤回机制。行使查询、更正、删除等数据权利，请联系 office@viyaf.org。
