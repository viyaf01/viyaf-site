Foundation Profile

About the Foundation

VIYAF is a Privatstiftung established and registered under Austria's Private Foundation Act (PSG). It pursues charitable purposes under its statutes and distributes no dividends; available funds and surpluses serve the charitable purpose.

Stiftungsvorstand · Board Member

Prof. Mag. Elzbieta Maria Wiedner-Zajac

International pianist · composer · educator (Vienna)

A pianist, composer and educator based in Vienna, she trained in Poland, the United States (University of Michigan, Ann Arbor) and Austria, and taught for many years at the University of Music and Performing Arts Vienna (mdw), where she obtained her Habilitation. She has appeared on leading European stages, including the Goldener Saal of the Vienna Musikverein, and her recordings and publications have earned high professional recognition.

She has received national honours from Austria — including the Austrian Cross of Honour for Science and Art — and from Poland. As a long-standing mdw educator and VIYAF board member, she brings stage standards, academic judgement and institutional credibility to the Foundation.

Finance & Governance

Three Separate Channels

Donations, sponsorship and program service fees use separate contracts, accounts, accounting bases and disclosures.

Donation / Spende

Entity: VIYAF Privatstiftung (Austria)

Donation Agreement / Impact Agreement. Supports young arts, scholarships, masterclasses and public-benefit projects. No investment return or income rights; tickets, dinners or other consideration are separated at fair value.

Sponsoring

Entity: Meta Management GmbH (Austria)

Sponsoring Agreement / Commercial Cooperation Agreement. Brand exposure, joint communications, client events and ESG/CSR content; commercial invoice, no donation receipt.

Program Fee

China-side entity: iMusic Union (Shenzhen) Cultural Technology Co., Ltd.

Program Participation Agreement. Student participation, masterclasses, travel, teaching rehearsals and competition training — a commercial service fee, not a VIYAF donation.
