How to Verify

Verify Independently in Three Steps

Match the legal name and number

Match the legal name and “FN 653499 d” in any document against this page (note the trailing letter “d”).
核对文件中的法定名称与「FN 653499 d」（注意编号末位含字母 d）是否与本页一致。

Obtain an official register excerpt

Anyone may obtain a Firmenbuch Auszug (official excerpt) independently through the Austrian justice system (JustizOnline), a notary or a lawyer.
任何人可通过奥地利司法系统（JustizOnline）、公证人或律师调取 Firmenbuch Auszug（登记摘录）独立核验。

Reconfirm via the official email

For payments or formal commitments, always reconfirm via office@viyaf.org; formal commitments require two board members’ joint signatures.
涉及款项或正式承诺，请务必通过 office@viyaf.org 二次确认；正式承诺以两名董事共同签署为准。

To request a Firmenbuch Auszug or compliance documentation from us, please email office@viyaf.org. 如需我方提供登记摘录或合规说明文件，请邮件联系。

Governance · Board (Stiftungsvorstand)

Board of Directors — Public Summary

Stiftungsvorstand · Board Member

Prof. Mag. Elzbieta Maria Wiedner-Zajac

International pianist · composer · educator (Vienna) · 钢琴家 · 作曲家 · 教育家

A pianist, composer and educator based in Vienna, she taught for many years at the University of Music and Performing Arts Vienna (mdw), where she obtained her Habilitation. She has appeared on leading European stages, including the Goldener Saal of the Vienna Musikverein, and her recordings and publications have earned high professional recognition. Her honours include the Austrian Cross of Honour for Science and Art as well as major Polish cultural and state decorations.

旅居维也纳的钢琴家、作曲家与教育家。曾在维也纳国立音乐与表演艺术大学（mdw）长期任教，并取得教授资格（Habilitation）。多次在维也纳音乐协会金色大厅等重要场馆演出；录音与出版作品获得专业领域高度评价。获奥地利共和国「科学与艺术荣誉十字」以及波兰共和国重要文化与国家勋章等荣誉。
